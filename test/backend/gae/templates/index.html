APNS Test App

This app lets you send messages to the APNS Test app installed on your iPhone. This is for demonstrating the power of the PushDaemon service.

First download the APNSTest app from the App Store. This is a free app and one with almost no practical use except to test the PushDaemon service.

Click below to visit a list of devices registered for push notification. You can send a message to any one of them!!!
